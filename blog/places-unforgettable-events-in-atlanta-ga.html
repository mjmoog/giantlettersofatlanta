Places for Unforgettable Events in Atlanta, GA

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Atlanta is a city that knows how to celebrate. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture, the Atlanta metro area offers an incredible variety of venues for every kind of event. Whether you are planning a wedding, a milestone birthday, a corporate gala, or a graduation party, the right venue sets the tone for the entire experience. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we have delivered our marquee letters to venues all across the city, and we have seen firsthand how the right location can transform an event from ordinary to extraordinary.

Historic Venues with Southern Charm

Atlanta is home to some of the most beautiful historic venues in the Southeast. Places like the Biltmore Ballrooms, with their stunning Spanish Baroque architecture, provide a sense of grandeur that pairs perfectly with giant illuminated letters. The Georgian Terrace Hotel, which has hosted movie premieres since 1911, offers elegant ballrooms with soaring ceilings that make marquee letters look even more impressive. For a more intimate setting, historic homes and gardens throughout Buckhead, Midtown, and Inman Park provide charming backdrops that blend Southern hospitality with modern celebration.

Outdoor Spaces and Gardens

Atlanta's mild climate makes outdoor events a popular choice for much of the year. The Atlanta Botanical Garden provides lush, manicured grounds that create a magical setting for weddings and special occasions. Piedmont Park, with its sweeping views of the Midtown skyline, offers open spaces perfect for large celebrations. Many private estates and farm venues in the north Georgia foothills, just a short drive from the city, combine rustic charm with elegant touches. Our marquee letters work beautifully in outdoor settings, and we provide stakes and blocks to keep them secure in any weather.

Modern Event Spaces

For those who prefer a contemporary aesthetic, Atlanta has no shortage of modern event venues. Converted warehouses in the Westside Provisions District and along the BeltLine offer industrial-chic spaces with exposed brick, high ceilings, and plenty of room for creative setups. Rooftop venues in Midtown and Downtown provide stunning city views that create a dramatic backdrop for evening celebrations. These clean, open spaces allow giant marquee letters to serve as the centerpiece of your event design.

Unique Atlanta Venues

Some of the most memorable events we have helped with took place at truly unique locations. Art galleries, breweries, and even the World of Coca-Cola have all served as unforgettable event settings. The Fox Theatre, with its Moorish architectural style, is one of the most photographed venues in the city. Restaurants with private dining rooms, boutique hotels, and community event halls throughout Decatur, Roswell, and Marietta round out the options for hosts looking for something special.

Choosing the Right Venue for Your Marquee Letters

When selecting a venue, consider the size of your display and the overall layout of the space. Indoor venues with high ceilings give our tall letters room to shine, while outdoor spaces allow for creative placement near entrances, dance floors, or photo areas. We handle delivery and setup at any venue in the greater Atlanta area, so all you need to worry about is enjoying your event. Reach out to us for a free quote and let us help you plan the perfect display for your chosen venue.
