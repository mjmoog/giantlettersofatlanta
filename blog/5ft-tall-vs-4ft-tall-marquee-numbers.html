5ft Tall vs 4ft Tall Marquee Numbers

Giant Letters of Atlanta

When it comes to marquee numbers and letters, size matters. One of the most common questions we get at Giant Letters of Atlanta is about the difference between various letter heights. While the marquee letter industry offers a range of sizes, two of the most popular are four-foot and five-foot tall characters. Here is a breakdown of the differences to help you decide which size is right for your event.

The Visual Impact of Height

The difference between four feet and five feet may not sound like much on paper, but when you are standing next to a marquee letter, that extra twelve inches makes a significant difference. Five-foot tall characters tower over most adults, creating a dramatic, larger-than-life presence that commands attention from across the room. Four-foot characters are still impressively large and stand about chest height on an average adult, making them substantial but slightly more understated.

Venue Considerations

The size of your venue should play a major role in your decision. In a large ballroom, banquet hall, or outdoor space, five-foot tall letters have room to breathe and make the impact they are designed to make. In a smaller venue, a cozy restaurant, or a home living room, four-foot letters might be a better fit because they fill the space without overwhelming it. Consider ceiling height as well. Most venues can accommodate five-foot characters with no problem, but if your venue has lower ceilings, the smaller size may be the way to go.

Photo Backdrop Potential

Both sizes work great as photo backdrops, but five-foot letters have an edge when it comes to group photos. Because they are taller than most people, they are fully visible even when a crowd gathers around them. Four-foot letters work perfectly for individual and couple photos, and they are especially popular for children's events where the characters are proportional to the younger guests.

Transportation and Setup

Larger letters require more space for transportation and setup. Five-foot characters need a vehicle with adequate cargo space and a clear path from the delivery vehicle to the setup location. Four-foot characters are somewhat easier to maneuver through narrow doorways and tight spaces. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we handle all transportation and setup regardless of size, so this is more of a consideration for the venue layout than something you need to worry about personally.

Making Your Decision

If you want maximum visual impact and you have the venue space to support it, go big with five-foot tall characters. If your space is more intimate or you prefer a look that is impressive but not overpowering, four-foot characters are an excellent choice. Either way, the glow, the craftsmanship, and the wow factor of giant marquee numbers will make your event unforgettable.
