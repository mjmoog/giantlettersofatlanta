Atlanta Ranks Top 5 Best Places to Get Married

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Atlanta has long been one of the most popular cities in the country for weddings, and it is not hard to see why. With its blend of Southern charm, world-class venues, beautiful weather, and a thriving vendor community, the city consistently ranks among the top five best places to get married in the United States. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we are proud to be part of the city's vibrant wedding scene, and we love helping couples create their perfect day.

Why Couples Choose Atlanta

There are countless reasons couples fall in love with Atlanta as a wedding destination. The city offers an incredible range of venues, from grand historic estates and elegant hotel ballrooms to rustic barns, rooftop terraces, and lush outdoor gardens. The cost of hosting a wedding in Atlanta is often more affordable than in cities like New York or Los Angeles, which means couples can stretch their budget further and invest in the details that matter most to them. And with Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport being one of the busiest in the world, it is easy for out-of-town guests to get here.

The Atlanta Wedding Experience

Atlanta weddings have a character all their own. There is a warmth and hospitality that permeates every celebration, from the rehearsal dinner to the last dance. Southern traditions like serving sweet tea, incorporating local cuisine, and making sure every guest feels like family create an atmosphere that is both elegant and welcoming. The city's diverse culture also means that Atlanta weddings come in every style imaginable, from classic and traditional to bold and contemporary.

Atlanta's World-Class Vendor Community

One of the things that sets Atlanta apart as a wedding destination is the depth and quality of its vendor community. From award-winning photographers and florists to talented caterers, DJs, and event planners, the city has no shortage of professionals who know how to make a wedding spectacular. Giant Letters of Atlanta is honored to be part of this community, and we love collaborating with other vendors to create cohesive, stunning celebrations.

Marquee Letters at Atlanta Weddings

Giant marquee letters have become a staple at Atlanta weddings. Couples use them to spell out "LOVE," "MR & MRS," their initials, or their last name, creating a focal point that doubles as a gorgeous photo backdrop. Our letters work beautifully at indoor receptions and outdoor ceremonies alike. There is nothing quite like seeing your name in lights on the most important day of your life. If you are planning a wedding in Atlanta, we would love to be part of your celebration.
