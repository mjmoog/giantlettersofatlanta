Giant Letters for Every Occasion

Giant Letters of Atlanta

One of the things we love most about our work at Giant Letters of Atlanta is the incredible variety of events we get to be part of. From romantic weddings to lively birthday bashes, elegant corporate galas to heartfelt family celebrations, our giant marquee letters have shown up at every kind of occasion you can imagine. Here is a roundup of our most popular rental combinations and the events they shine at.

Weddings: LOVE, I DO, MR & MRS

"LOVE" is our most-requested rental, and it is easy to see why. Four glowing letters that capture the entire spirit of a wedding day. "I DO" is a close second, often placed near the altar or ceremony backdrop. "MR & MRS" is a reception favorite, typically displayed behind the head table. Many couples also rent their initials, like "M & K," for a personalized touch that makes the display uniquely theirs.

Birthdays: Age Numbers and Names

Milestone birthday numbers are always in high demand. "30," "40," "50," and "21" are our top sellers. For kids' birthdays, parents love renting the child's name or the word "PARTY." Some hosts go all out with "HAPPY BIRTHDAY" in 2D letters, creating a massive display that wows every guest who walks through the door.

Graduations: GRAD, Class Year, School Initials

"GRAD" and the graduation year are graduation party staples. We also get a lot of requests for school initials or abbreviations like "GSU," "UGA," or "GT." These displays are perfect for photo opportunities and celebrating the graduate's achievement in a big way.

Proposals: MARRY ME

"MARRY ME" is one of the most emotional setups we do. There is nothing like watching someone's face light up when they see those seven giant letters glowing in front of them. We have set up proposal displays at parks, restaurants, rooftops, and living rooms across Atlanta, and every single one has been a yes.

Corporate Events: Company Names and Slogans

Businesses use our letters to spell out company names, product names, hashtags, and event themes at launches, galas, trade shows, and holiday parties. The professional look of our marquee letters elevates corporate events and provides excellent branding opportunities, especially when guests share photos on social media.

Baby Showers and Gender Reveals: BABY, OH BABY, BOY, GIRL

"BABY" and "OH BABY" are popular choices for baby showers, while "BOY" or "GIRL" are perfect for gender reveal parties. Some parents-to-be even rent the baby's name to announce it to family and friends at the shower.

Holidays and Seasonal Events

From "BOO" at Halloween parties to "JOY" and "NOEL" at Christmas celebrations, our letters help people celebrate every season. New Year's Eve parties frequently feature the upcoming year in giant numbers, counting down to midnight in style.
