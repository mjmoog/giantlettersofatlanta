Large Letter Rentals

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Renting giant marquee letters is one of the easiest and most impactful ways to elevate any event. Whether you are a first-time renter or you have used marquee letters before, this guide covers everything you need to know about the rental process, from choosing your letters to the day of your event. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we have made the process as simple and stress-free as possible so you can focus on what matters most: celebrating.

What Is a Large Letter Rental?

A large letter rental is exactly what it sounds like. You choose the letters, numbers, or special characters you want, we deliver them to your event venue, set them up, and pick them up when your event is over. Our letters stand several feet tall and are designed to be the visual centerpiece of your celebration. They are available in two styles: 2D flat letters and 3D illuminated letters, each offering a different look and feel.

Choosing the Right Style

Our 2D letters feature a clean, flat design with white fronts and black sides. They are a great choice for daytime events, outdoor celebrations, and anyone who prefers a modern, minimalist look. Our 3D letters feature built-in illumination that produces a warm, inviting glow. They are perfect for evening events, indoor receptions, and any occasion where you want that classic marquee ambiance. Both styles are available in the full alphabet, numbers 0 through 9, and special characters like the ampersand and hashtag.

How Pricing Works

Our pricing is straightforward and transparent. 2D letters are $60 per character per day, and 3D illuminated letters are $75 per character per day. Spaces between words do not count as characters, so you only pay for the letters and numbers you actually use. A refundable $50 deposit is required to secure your booking and is returned after the event. Delivery fees are based on the distance from our Woodstock, GA location to your venue.

The Rental Process

Renting from Giant Letters of Atlanta is simple. Start by requesting a free quote through our website. Tell us what letters or numbers you need, your event date, and your venue address. We will get back to you with pricing and availability. Once you confirm your booking and pay the deposit, your date is locked in. On the day of your event, we deliver the letters, set them up exactly where you want them, and return after the event to pick them up. You do not have to lift a finger.

Tips for a Great Rental Experience

Book early, especially during peak seasons like wedding season and graduation season, when our inventory fills up fast. Double-check the spelling of what you want displayed. Make sure your venue has accessible power outlets near where the letters will be placed. And communicate any special requirements, like outdoor placement or tight setup windows, so we can plan accordingly. We are always happy to answer questions and help you plan the perfect display.
