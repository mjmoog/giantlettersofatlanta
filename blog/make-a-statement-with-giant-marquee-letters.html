Make a Statement with Giant Marquee Letters

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Some events call for subtlety. Others call for a statement. When you want your celebration to be bold, memorable, and impossible to miss, giant marquee letters deliver in a way that few other decor elements can. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we specialize in helping our clients make the kind of statement that their guests will be talking about for years.

The Art of Making a Statement

Making a statement does not mean being loud or over the top. It means being intentional about what you want your event to communicate. A bride who displays "FOREVER" in giant illuminated letters is making a statement about her commitment. A company that spells out its name in glowing marquee letters at a launch event is making a statement about its brand. A family that lights up "50" for a milestone birthday is making a statement about the importance of the celebration. The letters themselves become a message that anchors the entire event.

Placement Is Everything

Where you position your marquee letters can dramatically change their impact. Placing them behind the head table at a wedding creates an elegant backdrop for the couple. Setting them up near the entrance of a party greets guests with excitement the moment they arrive. Positioning them on a dance floor turns them into the centerpiece of the evening. We always work with our clients to find the best placement for their specific venue and event layout to maximize visual impact.

Pairing Letters with Other Decor

Giant marquee letters look incredible on their own, but they can be elevated even further when paired with complementary decor. Balloon arches framing the letters, floral arrangements at their base, or a red carpet leading up to the display are all popular options. For evening events, the warm glow of the letters combined with candles, fairy lights, or uplighting creates an atmosphere that feels both glamorous and inviting.

Statement Ideas for Every Occasion

Need some inspiration? Here are some of our favorite statement-making letter combinations: "LOVE" or "I DO" for weddings, "CHEERS" for New Year's Eve parties, "BABY" for baby showers, "PROM" for school events, your company name for corporate gatherings, or the honoree's name or age for birthday celebrations. If you can spell it, we can light it up. And if you are not sure what to display, we are happy to help you brainstorm the perfect message for your event.
