Everyone Deserves to Be Loved

Giant Letters of Atlanta

At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we have had the honor of being part of some of the most heartfelt celebrations in the city. From surprise proposals to golden wedding anniversaries, from baby showers to adoption parties, one thing connects every event we serve: love. We believe that everyone deserves to be loved and that every expression of love deserves to be celebrated in a big, bright, beautiful way.

Love Comes in Many Forms

When people think of marquee letters spelling out "LOVE," they often picture a wedding. And while weddings are one of our most popular events, love is so much bigger than a single day. We have delivered letters for parents welcoming a new baby, for families celebrating a child's adoption, for couples marking twenty-five years together, and for friends throwing surprise parties for people they adore. Every one of these moments matters, and every one of them deserves to be celebrated with the same joy and enthusiasm.

Creating Moments That Matter

There is something powerful about seeing a word like "LOVE" standing five feet tall and glowing with warm light. It transforms a space and sets a tone that tells everyone present that this moment is special. We have seen couples tear up when they walk into a room and see their initials illuminated for their anniversary dinner. We have watched grandparents beam with pride at a family reunion with giant letters spelling out their last name. These are the moments that remind us why we do what we do.

Spreading Love in Atlanta

Atlanta is a city built on community, warmth, and genuine connection. From neighborhood block parties to grand celebrations at the finest venues in town, the people of this city know how to come together and show love. We are proud to play a small part in that tradition. Every time we set up our letters at an event, we are helping someone tell the people in their life that they matter, that they are seen, and that they are loved.

How Will You Celebrate Love?

Whether you are planning a romantic proposal, a family reunion, a birthday party for someone special, or any other celebration of the people who matter most to you, we would love to help you make it unforgettable. Our giant marquee letters are more than just decoration. They are a statement. They say, in the biggest and brightest way possible, that this person and this moment are worth celebrating. Because everyone deserves to be loved, and every celebration of love deserves to shine.
