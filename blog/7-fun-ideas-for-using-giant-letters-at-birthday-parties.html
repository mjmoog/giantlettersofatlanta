7 Fun Ideas for Using Giant Letters at Birthday Parties

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Birthday parties are all about making the guest of honor feel special, and nothing says "this party is for YOU" like giant marquee letters. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we have seen our letters used in all sorts of creative ways at birthday celebrations across the metro area. Here are seven of our favorite ideas to inspire your next party.

1. Display Their Age in Giant Numbers

This is the classic choice and it never gets old. Whether someone is turning 1 or 100, seeing their age displayed in giant glowing numbers is always a thrill. It makes a bold statement, creates an instant photo opportunity, and sets the tone for the entire celebration. Two-digit milestone ages like 21, 30, 40, and 50 are especially popular.

2. Spell Out Their Name

Make the birthday person feel like a star by spelling out their name in giant letters. This works especially well for kids' parties, sweet sixteens, and surprise parties. Imagine walking into a room and seeing your name glowing in lights. It is the ultimate personal touch.

3. Create a Photo Booth Backdrop

Set up the marquee letters as the centerpiece of a dedicated photo area. Add props like fun hats, oversized sunglasses, and themed accessories, and you have an instant photo booth that guests will line up for. The letters provide perfect lighting for photos and give every picture a polished, professional look.

4. Use Them as a Surprise Element

For surprise birthday parties, have the letters set up in the room before the guest of honor arrives. When they walk in and see "SURPRISE" or their age glowing in the dark, the reaction is priceless. We have helped coordinate many surprise setups, and the reveal moment is always magical.

5. Pair Them with a Theme

Whatever your party theme, marquee letters can enhance it. A Hollywood-themed party with the birthday person's name in lights. A disco party with numbers on the dance floor. A garden party with letters surrounded by flowers and greenery. The neutral white color of our letters means they complement any theme or color scheme.

6. Set Them Up Outdoors

Backyard birthday parties and outdoor celebrations are perfect settings for marquee letters. As the sun sets and the letters start to glow, they create a warm, festive atmosphere that transforms any outdoor space into a party venue. We provide stakes and blocks to keep the letters secure outdoors.

7. Combine Numbers and Words

Who says you have to choose? Combine the birthday person's age with a word for maximum impact. "FABULOUS 40" or "DIRTY 30" or "SWEET 16" adds personality and humor to the display. Get creative with your combination and make it reflect the guest of honor's personality.
