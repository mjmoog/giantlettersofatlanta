How Party Planners in Atlanta Are Cashing In

Giant Letters of Atlanta

The event planning industry in Atlanta is booming, and savvy party planners are finding creative ways to stand out in a competitive market. One of the biggest trends we have seen is event professionals partnering with marquee letter rental companies to offer their clients something truly special. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we work with dozens of planners across the metro area, and we have seen firsthand how adding marquee letters to their service offerings has helped them grow their businesses.

A Simple Add-On That Wows Clients

For event planners, one of the easiest ways to increase the value of every booking is to offer additional services that enhance the overall experience. Giant marquee letters are a perfect add-on because they require no extra work from the planner. We handle delivery, setup, and pickup. The planner simply includes our letters in their event package, and the result is a visually stunning display that makes the entire event look more polished and professional. Clients love it, and it often leads to glowing reviews and referrals.

Social Media Gold

In today's world, every event planner knows that social media visibility can make or break a business. Giant illuminated letters are incredibly photogenic, and when guests share photos on Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok, the planner's work gets seen by thousands of potential clients. Many of the planners we work with tell us that their most-shared and most-liked event photos feature our marquee letters. That kind of organic marketing is priceless.

Building a Premium Brand

Event planners who incorporate high-end decor elements like marquee letters into their offerings position themselves as premium service providers. This allows them to charge higher rates because they are delivering a more complete and visually impressive experience. Clients who are willing to invest in beautiful event decor are often willing to invest in higher-tier planning services as well. It is a win-win for both the planner and the client.
