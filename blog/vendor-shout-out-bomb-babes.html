Vendor Shout Out: Bomb Babes

Giant Letters of Atlanta

At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we believe that the best events are created when talented vendors come together. The Atlanta event industry is filled with passionate professionals who pour their hearts into making celebrations unforgettable. Today, we are excited to shine a spotlight on one of our favorite collaborators: Bomb Babes.

Who Are the Bomb Babes?

Bomb Babes is an Atlanta-based event vendor that has quickly earned a reputation for bringing energy, creativity, and style to every celebration they touch. Whether it is a birthday bash, a bridal shower, or a corporate event, the Bomb Babes team knows how to set the mood and keep the party going. Their attention to detail and commitment to making every client feel special is something we admire and share.

Why We Love Working Together

When Bomb Babes and Giant Letters of Atlanta join forces at an event, the results speak for themselves. Their creative styling and our giant illuminated letters create a visual experience that guests talk about long after the celebration ends. We have collaborated on events ranging from intimate birthday dinners to large-scale parties, and every time the combination of our services elevates the entire experience. There is something truly special about working with a team that shares your passion for making people happy.

Supporting Local Vendors

One of the things we love most about the Atlanta event community is the spirit of collaboration. Local vendors lift each other up, refer clients to one another, and work together to deliver experiences that none of us could create alone. When you hire local vendors like Bomb Babes and Giant Letters of Atlanta, you are investing in your community and supporting small businesses that genuinely care about your celebration.

Building Your Vendor Dream Team

If you are planning an event in Atlanta, we encourage you to build a team of local vendors who complement each other. Start with your venue, then layer in services like catering, entertainment, decor, and of course, giant marquee letters. Ask each vendor for recommendations, because the best referrals often come from professionals who have worked together before and know how to deliver a seamless experience. We are always happy to share our list of trusted Atlanta event partners.
