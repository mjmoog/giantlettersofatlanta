10 Unique Ways to Use Giant Illuminated Letters at Your Wedding

Giant Letters of Atlanta

Giant illuminated marquee letters have become a beloved element of modern weddings, and for good reason. They add warmth, personality, and a wow factor that few other decor elements can match. But if you think marquee letters are just for spelling "LOVE" behind the head table, think again. Here are ten unique ways to use them at your Atlanta wedding.

1. Welcome Guests at the Entrance

Set up the couple's initials or the word "WELCOME" at the entrance to your ceremony or reception venue. It sets the tone the moment guests arrive and creates an immediate photo opportunity before they even walk through the door.

2. Frame Your Ceremony Altar

Place "I DO" or your initials on either side of the altar to create a stunning ceremony backdrop. The letters add visual depth to your ceremony space and look gorgeous in photos as you exchange vows.

3. Light Up the Dance Floor

Position "DANCE" or "PARTY" near the dance floor to signal that it is time to celebrate. When the DJ drops the first song and the letters are glowing, your guests will know exactly where the party is.

4. Create a Photo Lounge

Set up a dedicated photo area with marquee letters, comfortable seating, and fun props. This gives guests a designated spot for photos throughout the evening and takes the pressure off a traditional photo booth.

5. Display Your New Last Name

After the ceremony, spell out your new shared last name at the reception. It is a beautiful way to celebrate the beginning of your new family and gives guests a visual reminder of the joyful union they just witnessed.

6. Mark the Cocktail Hour

Place "CHEERS" or "SIP SIP" in the cocktail hour area to add personality to the pre-reception gathering. It creates a fun atmosphere while guests mingle and enjoy their drinks.

7. Highlight the Sweetheart Table

If you are opting for a sweetheart table instead of a traditional head table, place "MR & MRS" or your initials directly behind your seats. The letters frame the couple perfectly and make the sweetheart table the visual centerpiece of the room.

8. Decorate the Send-Off Area

As you and your partner make your grand exit, have illuminated letters spelling "JUST MARRIED" or "FOREVER" along the send-off path. The glow of the letters in the evening creates a magical tunnel of light for your departure.

9. Use Them at the Rehearsal Dinner

Who says marquee letters are only for the wedding day? Set up your initials or "REHEARSAL" at the rehearsal dinner to kick off the wedding weekend in style. It shows your guests that every part of the celebration has been thoughtfully planned.

10. Surprise Your Partner

If one partner is handling the decor, surprise the other with a meaningful word or phrase displayed in giant letters. "FOREVER," "MY LOVE," or even an inside joke between the two of you adds an emotional, personal touch that your partner will never forget.
