How to Choose the Perfect Phrase for Your Giant Marquee Letter Display

Giant Letters of Atlanta

You have decided to rent giant marquee letters for your event. Great choice. Now comes the fun part: deciding what to spell. At Giant Letters of Atlanta, we help clients choose the perfect display every day, and we have learned a few things about what works best. Here are our top tips for selecting the right phrase, word, or number combination for your event.

Keep It Short and Impactful

The most powerful marquee letter displays tend to be short. Words like "LOVE," "BABY," "GRAD," and "CHEERS" pack a punch because they are immediately recognizable and communicate the spirit of the event in a single glance. Shorter displays also tend to look more balanced and are easier to photograph. As a general rule, aim for three to seven characters for maximum visual impact.

Consider Your Space

Think about where the letters will be displayed and how much room you have. A long phrase like "HAPPY BIRTHDAY" requires a wide wall or open area to display properly. If your venue has limited space, a shorter option like the birthday person's age or initials might work better. We are always happy to help you figure out what will fit in your specific venue.

Match the Occasion

The best displays feel like they belong at the event. Here are some popular choices organized by occasion:

  • Weddings: LOVE, I DO, MR & MRS, couple's initials, last name
  • Birthdays: Age numbers, the honoree's name, PARTY
  • Graduations: GRAD, class year, school initials
  • Baby showers: BABY, OH BABY, the baby's name
  • Proposals: MARRY ME
  • Corporate: Company name, hashtag, product name
  • Holidays: BOO, JOY, NOEL, the new year

Think About Photos

Your marquee letters will likely be one of the most photographed elements at your event. Choose a word or phrase that will look great in photos and communicate the event's purpose to anyone who sees the picture. When someone scrolls past the photo on social media, they should instantly understand what the celebration was about.

Add a Personal Touch

While classic choices like "LOVE" are popular for a reason, do not be afraid to get creative. Inside jokes, meaningful dates, nicknames, and unexpected words can make your display feel more personal and memorable. One of our favorite recent rentals was a couple who displayed "FINALLY" at their wedding after a long engagement. It made every guest laugh and perfectly captured their story.

Ask Us for Help

If you are stuck on what to display, just ask. We have helped hundreds of clients choose the perfect combination, and we love brainstorming ideas. Tell us about your event, and we will suggest options that fit your venue, budget, and vision. The right phrase can take your event from great to unforgettable.
