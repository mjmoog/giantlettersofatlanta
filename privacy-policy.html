Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you use our rental request form, we collect the information you provide including your name, email address, phone number, Instagram handle, event details, and delivery address. This information is used solely to process your rental request and communicate with you about your booking.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and fulfill your rental requests
  • Communicate with you about your bookings via phone, email, or SMS
  • Send you rental confirmations and updates
  • Improve our services

SMS Communications

By consenting to receive SMS messages from Giant Letters of Atlanta, you agree to receive text messages related to your rental booking. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business (such as our form processing service), as long as they agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
