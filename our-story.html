Our Story

How a father's love sparked a bright idea

It All Started With a Wedding

The story of how Giant Letters of Atlanta began is a heartwarming tale of a father's love and a daughter's wedding.

In 2018, Michael and Kim's daughter, Sarah, asked Michael to create 5ft tall marquee letters for her wedding. Sarah couldn't find a local company that offered what she was looking for — giant, beautifully illuminated letters that would make her special day truly unforgettable.

So Michael did what any dedicated father would do: he built them himself. The letters were a hit at Sarah's wedding, and guests couldn't stop talking about them. That's when Michael and Kim realized there was a real need for this service in Atlanta.

What started as a labor of love quickly grew into a thriving business. Today, Giant Letters of Atlanta is driven by a desire to help people celebrate and create lasting memories, as well as to support the success and profitability of small businesses throughout the Atlanta area.
